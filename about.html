About Us

AgroMitra is an innovative initiative helping Maharashtra farmers join the digital world and benefit from government schemes.

Our Mission

To empower farmers to enhance agriculture using digital tools, facilitate access to government schemes, and improve their financial situation using technology.

Our Vision

To create an agricultural system where every farmer is part of the digital world, where they can easily benefit from technology and adopt more sustainable and profitable farming practices.

What We Do

AgroMitra helps Maharashtra farmers in various ways, from guiding them into the digital world to helping them benefit from government schemes.
