About Our Portal

शेतकरी मित्रांनो, Welcome to AgroMitra - your complete digital companion for successful farming in Maharashtra.

We provide expert information on crops suitable for Maharashtra's climate, connect you with quality fertilizers, inform you about government schemes, and offer seasonal advice.

Our mission is to empower farmers with knowledge and resources to increase productivity and income.
